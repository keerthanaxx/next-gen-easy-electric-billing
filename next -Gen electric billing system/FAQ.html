Frequently Asked Questions

How do I calculate my electricity bill?
You can use our Electricity Bill Calculator page by entering your units to see an estimate of your bill instantly.
What payment methods do you accept?
We accept UPI, Credit/Debit Cards, Netbanking, and wallet payments like Paytm/PhonePe.
How can I download my bill?
After logging in, go to the 'My Bills' section and click 'Download PDF'.
Can I pay bills for multiple months together?
Yes, pending bills can be paid together without penalty if within the due date.
Who do I contact for incorrect billing?
Please contact our support team at support@example.com or call +91 98765 43210.
How can I update my contact details?
Log in to your account, go to the 'Profile' section, and click 'Edit' to update your email, phone number, or address.
What happens if I miss my bill due date?
If you miss the due date, a late payment fee will be added to your next bill. We recommend paying before the due date to avoid penalties.
Can I receive my bill via SMS or Email?
Yes! You can opt for e-bills by enabling notifications in your account settings under 'Communication Preferences'.
Where can I view my past bills?
All your previous bills can be accessed in the 'Billing History' section of your account dashboard after logging in.
